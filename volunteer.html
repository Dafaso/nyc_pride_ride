Volunteers Make It Happen!

OutCycling and the NYC Pride Ride rely on a team of volunteers to make our unique event a success.

  • Volunteers are needed for approximately 4-6 hour shifts on Sunday June 11th.
  • Volunteers with cars are especially needed to transport themselves/supplies to the rest stops along the route.
  • In addition to staffing rest stops along the route, we need volunteers for set up, crew registration/check-in, breakfast set up, finish line set up, stuffing gift bags, festival set up, food preparation and serving, and cheering the riders at the finish line.

For complete details, please fill out the form below and come to our volunteer orientation meeting on Thursday, June 8th at 6:30pm at We Work - 115 West 18th Street NYC. OutCycling members can RSVP for the orientation on the OutCycling website. We'll be serving pizza and refreshments and reviewing job descriptions and event timeline.

The OutCycling community needs you to help ensure a fun and safe event for everyone. Thank you for your interest and cooperation. We really appreciate it!
