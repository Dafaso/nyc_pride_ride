OUTCYCLING'S YOUTH CYCLING INITIATIVE

The Fearless Flyers Program.

  • Fearless Flyers Initiative is a youth cycling program for inner-city young adults ages 16-21
  • The program empowers young adults to build self-confidence, leadership and cycling skills.
  • It is a 10-week training program including safety training in partnership with Bike NY and the NYC DOT.
  • Each participant is provided a bike and helmet for the Saturday morning training rides.
  • Training rides start at 10 miles and gradually increase to finish with a 35 mile ride.
  • Graduates who successfully complete the training earn a new bike, helmet, and jersey.

How You Can Help.

OutCycling is seeking support to strengthen and grow the Youth Initiative for the 2017 season. Institutional or Individual support in the form of donations, grants or sponsorships is being sought to help build and expand the program.
